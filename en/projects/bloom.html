A platform to reimagine live events

Bloom is a digital product created and produced by D3 that changes the way we organize, sponsor and enjoy live events.

For organizers, brands and users

Bloom helps organizers to be more efficient, sponsor brands to be more relevant and users to experience more.

Photo: D3

For users, the live event experience is seamless, easier and even more enjoyable

It is possible to buy tickets, pay for drinks and check in using only the cell phone.

Photo: D3

For organizers, all in one platform

Bloom captures, organizes and accumulates all data to be richly visualized in a series of interfaces specifically designed to enable business insights.

Photo: D3

Personalized experiences for each consumer

Bloom helps reducing the friction in marketing actions of sponsoring brands. For the Oi action at Rock in Rio we created an image sharing system that had conversion over 92%.

Photo: D3

D3 developed the whole hardware, software and network infrastructure

It supports several types of contactless technologies and have a reliable and fast wireless communication even in noisy environments.

Photo: D3

Rock in Rio 2015

Bloom rocked in Rock in Rio in 2015: In 7 days of event we served almost 100 thousand people and saved thousands of hours of all attractions queues.
